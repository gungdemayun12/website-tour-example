North Bali Tour

North Bali is a serene and tranquil region, offering a mix of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and unique attractions. From breathtaking waterfalls to peaceful lakes, the region is a haven for nature enthusiasts and those looking to explore Bali's less crowded gems.

Banyumala Waterfall

Banyumala Waterfall is a hidden gem located in the lush greenery of North Bali. Known for its twin cascades and pristine natural pools, it is the perfect spot for those looking to immerse themselves in the serenity of nature. The short trek to the waterfall takes you through a picturesque jungle path, offering stunning views along the way. Visitors can swim in the cool, clear waters or simply relax and take in the tranquil atmosphere of this beautiful location.

Twin Lake

Danau Kembar, or the Twin Lakes (Buyan and Tamblingan), are two scenic lakes surrounded by dense forest and rolling hills. These lakes are part of a protected nature reserve and are known for their untouched beauty and serene environment. Visitors can take a traditional canoe ride, explore the surrounding trekking paths, or simply enjoy the breathtaking views. The area is also home to various bird species, making it a great spot for birdwatching enthusiasts.

Munduk Waterfall

Munduk Waterfall is one of the most famous waterfalls in Bali, surrounded by coffee and clove plantations. The cascading water is crystal clear, creating a refreshing natural pool at its base. The area is a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers due to its scenic trails and lush greenery. As you trek through the area, you can also explore smaller waterfalls and enjoy the cool, misty climate of North Bali.

Lovina Beach

Lovina is a coastal area famous for its black sand beaches and tranquil ambiance. It is best known for its dolphin-watching tours at sunrise, offering visitors the chance to see pods of dolphins swimming in their natural habitat. The area also features vibrant coral reefs, making it a great spot for snorkeling and diving. Lovina's laid-back vibe, combined with its cultural attractions and natural beauty, makes it a perfect destination for relaxation and exploration.
